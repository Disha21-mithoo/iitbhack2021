ABOUT THE HACKATHON

At I_Hack 2021, we bring together India’s best coders, developers, designers, innovators, creators and entrepreneurs of tomorrow. I_Hack is geared up for those who are passionate about building, designing and innovating. It is a one of its kind tech challenge. I_Hack comprises of 2 parallel tracks :



TRUMINDS AI TRACK

AI is poised to drive innovation by accelerating the development of new products. According to Elon Musk, robots will end up moving so fast that one will need a strobe light just to see them. We firmly believe that he might just be onto something great and are pleased to present the Truminds AI Track which will bring together innovative data scientists and developers from across the ecosystem to solve real world challenges by applying AI and machine learning. This track is sponsored by Truminds




WIPRO SOFTWARE TRACK, IN COLLABORATION WITH TOPCODER

Can't control the geeky entrepreneurial side of yours? always wanted to build great tech and optimize things, but always were a little confused where to start with? We have something for you as well. The Software Track will have a pre-defined problem statement for which hackers will have to come up with their best solution in 30 hours and the most efficient and practical solution will be the winner. This track is sponsored by Wipro.
